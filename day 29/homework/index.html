GOA ORIENTED ACADEMY


GOA

(Goal-Oriented Academy) is an educational institution based in Georgia, offering programs in programming, IT technologies, web development, UX/UI design, and other modern disciplines. The academy focuses on practical skill development and professional growth, catering to beginners and professionals alike. Courses are available both online and in-person, with regular enrollment for new cohorts, such as the September intake. GOA emphasizes hands-on learning and career-ready skills in technology and creative fields.

My addiction to Goa

Entering Goa was a great happiness for me because I wanted to study programming at a professional level and I was looking for a school where I was taught programming at a high level and I found Goa where learning is very good and fun.At first, I was a little afraid, but when I got used to the space and made friends, a greater need for learning arose As for the mentors, they are professionals in their field and do their best to teach you the material

Hackathons are also held once a month, which is very interesting and pleasant
