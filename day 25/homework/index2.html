"Rush Hour" is a 1998 American action-comedy film directed by Brett Ratner. The main roles are played by Jackie Chan and Chris Tucker. The movie revolves around Detective Lee (Jackie Chan), who travels from Hong Kong to Los Angeles to assist American police officer James Carter (Chris Tucker) in a case. Despite their differences and comedic clashes, the two work together to tackle a crime and solve a major problem.
