Teaching in Goa

Currently, we have over 1,000 online students, all of whom are 100% engaged and happy with their learning experience. (This is not an exaggeration—you’ll see it for yourself!) The minimum age requirement is 8-9 years old, and we teach programming from scratch. Additionally, we help students enhance their logic, math, and English skills along the way. Lessons include 10-minute breaks with classical music or physical exercises to keep students refreshed and engaged. Unlimited Mentoring Our service also includes unlimited individual mentoring sessions, ensuring that every student fully understands the material and progresses confidently.
