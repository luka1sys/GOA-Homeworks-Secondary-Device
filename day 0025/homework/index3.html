"Night at the Museum" is a 2006 American family comedy film directed by Shawn Levy. The main role is played by Ben Stiller, who portrays Larry Daley, a man who becomes the new night guard at a museum and encounters unusual and amazing events when the museum exhibits come to life at night. The film, suitable for a young audience, is set in a museum where Larry discovers that, at night, the exhibits and statues come to life, and he has to restore order in the museum. The movie features humorous scenes and memorable characters, including Teddy Roosevelt (played by Robin Williams), and other historical figures.
